Final Year Project

My final year project revolved around the research of surveillance capitalism and the creation of a series of artworks in response to surveillance capitalist practices.

This is the fourth and final artowrk in said series. Unfortunately, due to the restrictions surrounding Covid-19, this piece could not have been actualised within a physical space.
For this reason, the concept as well as a demonstration have been layed out in a video format.
