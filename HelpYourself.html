Help Yourself

'Help Yourself' was an interactive artwork displayed for one week in the UL, CSIS atrium. Created in collaboration with Patrick O'Donnell, the piece seeks to critique the unsustainable practices of palm oil production.

The work was created using a laser printer, Arduino board and circuitry, as well as the programs Processing and After Effects.
