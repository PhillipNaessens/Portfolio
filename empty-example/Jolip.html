Jolip

'Jolip' was a collaborative live coding group formed by Jodie Noone and I as part of a creative coding module. They were tasked with creating an audio/visual live coding set to be performed in front CSIS students and staff.

Jodie was in control of the visuals while I was responsible for the audio.
