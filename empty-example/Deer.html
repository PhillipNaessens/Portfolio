Desgarro (XLIV) Music Video

For this assignment I was tasked with creating a one minute long music video for a royalty free piece of music. The chosen piece was 'Desgarro (XLIV)' by Opo.

The loose theme of the video being climate change was inspired by the synthesis of both guttural singing and screetching electronics present in the song.
